## ­Ъџђ Core Philosophy: Frictionless Security

Traditional cybersecurity tools often create hurdles (complex logins, wallet connections, gas fees). SecureAI Guardian removes these barriers through two innovative architectural choices:

### 1. Neural Passport (Identity 2.0)
Instead of passwords, the system uses **Cryptographic Device Bonding**. 
*   **Initialization:** Upon first entry, the system provisions a unique **Forensic UID**.
*   **Bonding:** This identity is cryptographically bonded to your local environment.
*   **Seamless Access:** You don't "log in"; your device is your key. Returning to the app instantly restores your clearance level and forensic ledger.

### 2. Managed Solana Relay
Blockchain verification is critical for immutability, but wallet management is a friction point.
*   **Shadow-Signers:** Every account is mapped to a system-managed shadow-signer on the Solana network.
*   **Zero-Gas UX:** The app handles all transaction signatures and fees (covered by the platform relay), providing a "Certified Block" signature for every scan without requiring a browser extension.
